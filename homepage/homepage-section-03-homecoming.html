The Signature Journey moves through four of South Africa's most extraordinary provinces, from the living history of Johannesburg and Soweto, to private game drives at Kapama River Lodge, the warm coastline of KwaZulu-Natal, and the breathtaking landscapes of Cape Town. Intentionally intimate and anchored by five signature moments, every stop is experienced with privileged local access and every detail taken care of from the moment you land. For those who wish to linger, an optional Winelands extension awaits. This is South Africa as most travelers never see it.

  • Itinerary at a Glance: Gauteng, Mpumalanga, KwaZulu-Natal, and the Western Cape.
