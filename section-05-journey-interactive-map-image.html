The Route

Journey Across
Four Provinces

Your Signature Journey moves from the living history of Johannesburg and Soweto to the ancient landscapes of Mpumalanga, the warm coastline of KwaZulu-Natal, and the creative energy of Cape Town.

Each province is explored with privileged local access, private game drives, and intimate hosted moments. Five of these are our Signature Moments: experiences chosen because they bring you closer to the country than any standard itinerary can.

Gauteng

Immerse Yourself in the Living History of South Africa

Walk through the powerful exhibits of the Apartheid Museum and experience Soweto with community rooted guides who know these streets intimately. Visit Mandela House on Vilakazi Street, the Hector Pieterson Memorial, and Freedom Park for a perspective on South Africa's past that goes far beyond the surface.

Signature MomentA hosted lunch in Soweto, joined by a special guest from the generation that shaped the city's history.

Mpumalanga

Into the Wild. Where the Landscape Takes Over.

Travel north into Mpumalanga along the Panorama Route, where the landscape opens dramatically at Blyde River Canyon, God's Window, and Lisbon Falls. Then settle into a private game reserve for exclusive game drives, bush walks, and conservation experiences. The Big Five, on your own terms.

Signature MomentPrivate sundowners in the bush. An evening stop in the reserve as the light goes, with drinks and the quiet that only the bush has.

KwaZulu-Natal

The Warmth of the Zulu Kingdom

Arrive on the warm coastline of KwaZulu-Natal and venture into an authentic Zulu cultural experience rooted in genuine local access. Spend a full day at iSimangaliso Wetland Park, one of Africa's most biodiverse ecosystems, with a boat cruise through the waterways of St Lucia.

Western Cape

Cape Town. Where the Continent Ends.

Cape Town rewards those who take their time. Drive the Cape Peninsula, access Robben Island with rare insider access, and immerse yourself in the city's world-class contemporary African art scene. Wind the Atlantic Seaboard in a vintage car convoy along one of the world's most spectacular coastlines. Then, on your final morning, take a scenic helicopter flight over Table Mountain, the Twelve Apostles, and Robben Island. The journey closes unforgettably.

Signature MomentsCreative access. A private visit to an artist's studio, designer, or gallery, with the people shaping South Africa's creative present.
Farewell dinner at Gold Restaurant. A private venue, a South African feast, and live Djembe drumming to close the journey.
